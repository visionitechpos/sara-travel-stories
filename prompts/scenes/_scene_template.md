# Scene Prompt Template

## How to Use

Copy this template, fill in the fields, and use the output as your scene generation prompt.

---

## Template

```
LOCATION: [Specific location name, city, country]

ENVIRONMENT DESCRIPTION:
[2-4 sentences describing the specific location — architecture, textures, colors, scale]

TIME OF DAY: [Golden hour AM / Golden hour PM / Overcast / Night]

LIGHTING: [Describe the specific quality of light for this scene]

KEY VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- [Element 1]
- [Element 2]
- [Element 3]

SARA'S POSITION: [Where is Sara in the frame]
SARA'S ACTION: [What is she doing — walking, looking, standing, talking]
SARA'S EXPRESSION: [Emotional tone for this moment]

SHOT TYPE: [Wide / Medium / Close / Over-shoulder]
CAMERA ANGLE: [Eye level / Slightly low / High]

MOOD: [Overall mood of the scene]

STYLE: Photorealistic, cinematic quality, natural light, authentic.
```

---

## Example: Al-Balad Alley Shot

```
LOCATION: Al-Balad historic district, Jeddah, Saudi Arabia

ENVIRONMENT DESCRIPTION:
Narrow alley between old coral-stone buildings, 3-4 stories tall.
Wooden Rawasheen (latticed bay windows) project outward from upper floors.
Walls are weathered ochre and sand-white. Faded paint, old iron doors.
The alley is slightly shaded — patches of warm morning light fall on the walls.

TIME OF DAY: Golden hour AM

LIGHTING: Low angled morning light creating long shadows and warm tones.
Patches of direct light on wall textures. Sara in partial shade.

KEY VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Old wooden Rawasheen above
- Weathered coral-stone walls
- Worn stone floor
- An old iron door to one side

SARA'S POSITION: Center-left of frame, alley receding behind her
SARA'S ACTION: Walking slowly, running her hand along the wall
SARA'S EXPRESSION: Curious, quietly absorbed

SHOT TYPE: Medium
CAMERA ANGLE: Eye level

MOOD: Quiet discovery, connection to the past

STYLE: Photorealistic, cinematic quality, natural light, authentic.
```
