# Scene Prompts — Jeddah Corniche

## About This Location

The Jeddah Corniche is a long coastal promenade along the Red Sea.

Wide open views, sea breeze, palm trees, the King Fahd Fountain visible from many points.
People walking, families, movement. Best light at golden hour.

Reference images: `/references/jeddah/corniche/`

---

## Scene A — Early Morning Walk

```
LOCATION: Jeddah Corniche, Red Sea waterfront, Saudi Arabia

ENVIRONMENT DESCRIPTION:
Wide paved promenade along the Red Sea. Palm trees line the walkway.
The sea is calm in the early morning, deep blue-green.
A few early walkers in the distance. King Fahd Fountain visible far right.
Clean air, open sky.

TIME OF DAY: Golden hour AM

LIGHTING: Low warm morning light from the east. Sea reflects warm tones.
Long shadows from palm trees across the promenade.

KEY VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Wide open Red Sea horizon
- Palm tree shadows on the promenade
- King Fahd Fountain in background
- Calm water surface

SARA'S POSITION: Walking toward camera, slightly to the right of center
SARA'S ACTION: Walking at a relaxed pace, looking out toward the sea
SARA'S EXPRESSION: Peaceful, at ease — this is home

SHOT TYPE: Medium wide
CAMERA ANGLE: Eye level

MOOD: Calm, familiar, open. This is where Sara starts from.

STYLE: Photorealistic, cinematic, warm golden morning light, authentic.
```

## Scene B — Looking at the Sea

```
LOCATION: Jeddah Corniche seafront railing, Saudi Arabia

ENVIRONMENT DESCRIPTION:
Simple metal railing at the edge of the Corniche. The Red Sea stretches to the horizon.
Late afternoon light turns the water copper and gold.
The city is behind Sara — she faces the open sea.

TIME OF DAY: Golden hour PM

LIGHTING: Warm backlight from the setting sun. Sea is lit in copper and gold.
Sara is slightly silhouetted — face visible but warm.

KEY VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Open sea stretching to horizon
- Metal railing in foreground
- Copper and gold light on water
- Wide open sky

SARA'S POSITION: Leaning slightly on the railing, facing the sea
SARA'S ACTION: Still. Looking at the horizon.
SARA'S EXPRESSION: Quiet reflection. A moment before something.

SHOT TYPE: Medium, from the side
CAMERA ANGLE: Eye level

MOOD: Pause. Threshold. About to go somewhere or just returned.

STYLE: Photorealistic, cinematic, golden hour backlight, authentic.
```
