# Scene Prompts — Al-Balad, Jeddah

## About This Location

Al-Balad is the historic district of Jeddah. UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Coral-stone buildings, wooden Rawasheen (latticed bay windows), narrow alleys.
Over 1,000 years of continuous habitation.

Reference images: `/references/jeddah/al-balad/`

## Scene A — The Alley Arrival

```
LOCATION: Al-Balad historic district, Jeddah, Saudi Arabia

ENVIRONMENT DESCRIPTION:
Narrow alley between old coral-stone buildings, 5-7 stories tall with a narrow profile.
The height creates a canyon-like feeling — sunlight only reaches the alley floor at certain times.
Wooden Rawasheen project outward from upper floors, casting latticed shadows.
Walls are weathered ochre and dusty white. Old iron doors and faded paint.
The alley curves slightly ahead — the end is not visible.
A distant sound of daily life — a door closing, someone calling from above.

TIME OF DAY: Golden hour AM

LIGHTING: Low angled morning light. Warm golden tones.
Deep shadow in the alley depths. Patches of direct light on upper walls.

KEY VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Rawasheen latticed bay windows
- Weathered coral-stone texture
- Worn stone floor
- The curve of the alley disappearing ahead

SARA'S POSITION: Entering the frame from the left, mid-alley
SARA'S ACTION: Walking slowly, looking upward at the buildings
SARA'S EXPRESSION: Wonder, quiet curiosity

SHOT TYPE: Medium wide
CAMERA ANGLE: Eye level

MOOD: First discovery. Old world. Human scale against ancient structures.

STYLE: Photorealistic, cinematic, natural warm light, authentic.
```

## Scene B — Old Doorway

```
LOCATION: Al-Balad historic district, Jeddah, Saudi Arabia

ENVIRONMENT DESCRIPTION:
Sara stands before a large ornate wooden door, heavily carved.
The door is set into a coral-stone archway. Faded blue and green paint.
Wall texture is rough and weathered. A small window with iron grille above the door.

TIME OF DAY: Morning, slightly overcast

LIGHTING: Soft even light, no harsh shadows. Colors muted and warm.

KEY VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Ornate carved wooden door
- Coral-stone archway
- Faded paint on surrounding wall
- Iron grille above door

SARA'S POSITION: Slightly left of center, facing the door at three-quarter angle
SARA'S ACTION: Studying the door, perhaps touching the carved detail
SARA'S EXPRESSION: Focused, thoughtful

SHOT TYPE: Medium
CAMERA ANGLE: Eye level, slightly low to give door and architecture presence

MOOD: Respect for age and craft. Quiet contemplation.

STYLE: Photorealistic, cinematic, natural soft morning light, authentic.
```

## Scene C — Rooftop View

```
LOCATION: Al-Balad rooftop, Jeddah, Saudi Arabia

ENVIRONMENT DESCRIPTION:
Old rooftop looking out over Al-Balad's roofscape.
Irregular rooflines, 5-7 story buildings with Rawasheen projecting at different heights.
In the distance, glimpse of modern Jeddah skyline contrasting with the old city below.
Low stone parapet, worn but solid. Satellite dishes and water tanks on nearby rooftops — signs of a neighborhood still in active use.

TIME OF DAY: Golden hour PM

LIGHTING: Warm late afternoon light. Long shadows across rooftops.
Sky is golden-blue gradient.

KEY VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Old rooftop texture underfoot
- Irregular roofscape stretching ahead
- Distant modern skyline as contrast
- Wide open sky

SARA'S POSITION: Standing at the parapet, facing away from camera
SARA'S ACTION: Looking out over the city
SARA'S EXPRESSION: Reflective (seen from behind — body language conveys it)

SHOT TYPE: Wide
CAMERA ANGLE: Slightly behind and above Sara

MOOD: Contemplative. The weight of time. Old and new in one frame.

STYLE: Photorealistic, cinematic, golden hour light, authentic.
```
