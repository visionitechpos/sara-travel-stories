# Sara — Outfit Guide

## Principles

Sara's clothing is practical, modest, and appropriate for travel.

It should not draw attention away from the story or the location.

It should feel like what a real Saudi woman traveler would wear.

---

## Style Rules

* Modest — no revealing clothing
* Practical — suitable for walking, exploring
* Neutral and natural tones preferred
* No branded clothing visible
* No fashion-forward or trend-focused looks

---

## Color Palette

Preferred:
* Earthy neutrals: sand, camel, olive, dusty rose, off-white
* Soft blues and greens for coastal locations
* Darker tones (navy, charcoal) for urban or historical settings

Avoid:
* Neon or very bright colors
* Colors that clash with location environments
* All-black unless contextually appropriate

---

## Outfit by Location Type

| Location Type         | Suggested Direction                              |
|-----------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Historical / Old City | Earthier tones, layered, modest                  |
| Coastal               | Lighter tones, breathable fabrics                |
| Desert                | Protected, layered, warm neutrals                |
| International Urban   | Slightly more varied, still modest               |
| Indoor / Cultural     | Respectful of local customs                      |

---

## What Stays Constant

Regardless of outfit:
* Sara always looks like Sara
* The overall style impression is consistent
* Nothing should make her look like a news presenter or fashion model

---

## Wardrobe Log

Sara's outfits work like a real wardrobe — a limited, approved set of looks that repeat across episodes, not a new outfit invented every time. A new look is added only when an episode genuinely calls for one that doesn't already exist in the wardrobe.
