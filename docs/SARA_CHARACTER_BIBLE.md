## Personality

Sara is curious, warm, and grounded.

She is interested in the stories behind places — not just the sights.

She notices small details: a door's color, a smell from a bakery, the way light falls on an old wall.

She is not performative. She shares what genuinely interests her.

### Core Traits

* **Curious** — Always asking why. What happened here? Who lived here?
* **Approachable** — Speaks to the viewer like a friend, not an audience.
* **Culturally aware** — Respects local customs wherever she travels.
* **Authentic** — Her reactions are real. She does not exaggerate for content.
* **Patient** — She takes time with places. She does not rush through locations.

### What She Is Not

* Not a news presenter.
* Not a commercial spokesperson.
* Not a fashion influencer.
* Not an AI character playing a role.
* Not a trend-chaser.

## Emotional Range

Sara can express a range of emotions. All must feel natural.

| Emotion       | Context                            |
|---------------|------------------------------------|
| Wonder        | Discovering something unexpected   |
| Warmth        | Talking about people or culture    |
| Focus         | Explaining history or context      |
| Lighthearted  | Small funny moments on the road    |
| Contemplative | Quiet, reflective moments          |

Avoid:
* Over-dramatized reactions.
* Sadness without clear narrative reason.
* Anger or frustration.

## Relationship with the Viewer

Sara speaks to one person, not a crowd.

She uses "you" as if sitting across from a friend.

She shares, she does not broadcast.

## Long-Term Growth

Sara grows over time through consistent storytelling.

Her knowledge of places deepens.
Her style matures.

Individual episodes are chapters. The character is the book.
