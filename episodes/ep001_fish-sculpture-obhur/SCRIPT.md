## Language Review Notes

Reviewed against `SARA_LANGUAGE_BIBLE.md` (all five layers) across multiple passes during development:

- هذي → هادي (default feminine demonstrative)
- مش → مو (Egyptian negation replaced)
- هالـ contraction (هالمكان، هالسمكة) → هادا/هادي separated (project-wide rule now)
- نظرت → شفت (too literary for casual observation)
- يعرفون → يعرفو (Hejazi drops final ن on plural verbs)
- مثل → زي (preferred vocabulary)
- Structural: demonstrative repositioned to stay adjacent to noun+adjective (السمكة الكبيرة هادي, not السمكة هادي الكبيرة) and to follow a full إضافة phrase (قصة السمكة هادي, not قصة هادي السمكة)

**Scope note applied:** the bystander in the Body section is not required to speak Hejazi (`SARA_LANGUAGE_BIBLE.md` scope note) — his dialect in final voice production may reflect Jeddah's real demographic diversity.
